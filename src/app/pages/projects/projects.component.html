This is a demonstration of the MiniMax algorithm. The MiniMax algorithm is a recursive method that evaluates the best move in a game by assigning scores to the possible outcomes. It constructs a decision tree where each node represents a potential move, with leaves maximizing or minimizing the score based on whose turn it is (the maximizing player or the minimizing player).
