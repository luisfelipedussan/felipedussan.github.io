About

Luis Felipe is a Colombian based creative, with an eye for capturing not only the country of Colombia he calls home, but nearly every other beautiful location the world has to offer.
His passions reside on photography, music, literature, art, extreme sports and Artificial Intelligence.
His work is inspired by disciplines like philosophy, physics, religion, mathematics and biology.
He concluded his studies in computer science at Universidad de los Andes, where he developed his skills in algorithms, engineering, data structures, mathematics, programming languages and others.
